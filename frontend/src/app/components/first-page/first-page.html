Bauerblick was born at TU Wien as a university project with a simple idea â€” what if managing a farm could feel as intuitive and engaging as playing a game?

Currently available across Austria, you can position your farm anywhere in the country using real coordinates. Based on your location, field type, live weather data and strong database about the different seed types, Bauerblick provides personalized alerts and recommendations to support your farming journey.
